About SwiftApply

SwiftApply is an innovative tool designed to streamline the job application process using automation powered by Puppeteer. Whether you're applying to dozens of positions or just a few, SwiftApply helps you save time and stay organized.

How It Works

SwiftApply automates the repetitive tasks involved in job applications:
  • Fills out application forms with your pre-saved data.
  • Handles various form formats with dynamic adaptability.
  • Ensures a seamless application process across multiple platforms.

Advantages

  • Time-Saving: Apply to multiple jobs quickly.
  • Consistency: Minimize human error by automating data entry.
  • Customizable: Tailor your application details easily.

Getting Started

Follow these simple steps to set up SwiftApply:
  1. Download and install the software from our official repository.
  2. Configure your application preferences in the settings panel.
  3. Run SwiftApply and watch it automate your job applications!
